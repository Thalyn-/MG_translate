How to mine Bytecoin, Monero, QuazarCoin, Litecoin, duckNote, Fantomcoin, MonetaVerde, Aeon coin, Dashcoin, Infinium-8 on Windows?

  1. Sign up on minergate.com
  2. Download miner suitable for Windows on minergate.com/downloads/gui
  3. Install miner
  4. Launch miner and sign in using your account credentials
  5. Choose number of cores you would like to use for mining and press start under coin
